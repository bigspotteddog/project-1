Our Story

Sweet & Savory was born from a simple idea: why not enjoy the best of both worlds?

Founded in 2018, our shop combines the sweet joy of artisan donuts with the savory satisfaction of gourmet sandwiches. We believe in using only the finest ingredients, supporting local producers, and creating a welcoming space for our community.

Every item is made with care, creativity, and a commitment to quality that you can taste in every bite.
